If you subscribe from the iOS app, Apple manages the purchase and renewal. CalBudget shows your plan in the app, but billing changes for App Store purchases are handled through your Apple account.

## Manage an iOS subscription

To manage a subscription purchased on iPhone:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your Apple ID.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **CalBudget**.
5. Review, change, or cancel the subscription.

## If web and iOS show different plans

Make sure you are signed in to CalBudget with the same account on both devices. If your iOS purchase does not appear in the web app:

1. Refresh CalBudget.
2. Sign out and sign back in on both devices.
3. Confirm the email or Apple sign-in identity you used.
4. Contact support from the in-app support widget.

Apple receipts do not include your private payment credentials. If support asks for purchase context, share the purchase email, receipt date, and plan name.

## Restoring access

If you reinstall the iOS app or change devices, sign in with the same CalBudget account you used before. Then check **Billing and plan** or the iOS subscription screen.
