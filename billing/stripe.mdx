Web subscriptions for CalBudget are managed from **Billing and plan** in the profile menu.

## What you can manage

From billing, you can:

- View your current plan.
- Upgrade from **Free** to **Plus** or **Advisor**.
- Switch between available billing periods.
- Update your payment method.
- Review renewal timing.
- Open the billing portal when available.
- Cancel a paid subscription.

## Canceling a web subscription

If you cancel a paid subscription, access normally continues through the end of the paid billing period. After that, your account returns to the access level available on your plan.

If you return to **Free**, plan limits may apply again. Existing data is not a recommendation to delete information; it simply means some actions may require upgrading again.

## If your plan looks wrong

If you paid but still see **Free**:

1. Refresh the app.
2. Sign out and sign back in.
3. Confirm you are using the same email address that purchased the plan.
4. Open **Billing and plan** from the profile menu.
5. Contact support from the in-app support widget or email [support@calbudget.com](mailto:support@calbudget.com).

Include the email address on the CalBudget account and whether the purchase was made on web or iOS. Do not send full card numbers or private payment credentials.

## Invoices and receipts

Receipts are usually sent to the email address associated with the purchase. If you need help finding a receipt, contact support and include the purchase email address.
