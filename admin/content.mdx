CalBudget has several content surfaces.

## Blog

Blog posts live in the database and can be:

- Draft
- Scheduled
- Published

Published posts appear on `/blog` when status is published and the published date is not in the future. Scheduled posts can be used as a content queue.

## Changelog

Changelog entries announce product updates. They can also power in-app release announcements.

## Help content

Help center content explains specific workflows. Keep help articles practical and user-facing.

## SEO pages

Marketing SEO pages explain related concepts such as budget calendars, cash-flow planning, running balance, and bill calendars.

## Content checklist

- Use clear titles.
- Keep one H1 per page.
- Add useful excerpts and metadata.
- Avoid fabricated testimonials or claims.
- Link related guides.
- Keep plan and pricing details current.
