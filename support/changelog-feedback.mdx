CalBudget changes over time. Product updates explain what changed, and feedback helps shape what should improve next.

## Latest updates

Open the question-mark menu in the platform and choose **Latest Updates** to see recent product changes.

Updates may include:

- New features.
- Design improvements.
- Billing or plan changes.
- Mobile app updates.
- Bug fixes.
- Workflow improvements.

## Share feedback

Use in-app feedback or support when you want to report:

- A confusing workflow.
- A bug.
- A missing feature.
- A billing question.
- An onboarding problem.
- A mobile or desktop issue.

## Good feedback includes

- What you expected to happen.
- What actually happened.
- Which device or browser you used.
- Screenshots with private details hidden when possible.

## Replay onboarding help

If you want to review how the platform works, open settings and replay the walkthrough when available.
