CalBudget support is available through docs, the in-app support widget, and email.

## Help center

The public help center is:

[https://docs.calbudget.com](https://docs.calbudget.com)

Use the help center when you want to learn a workflow, understand a feature, or troubleshoot common issues.

## Contact support from the app

Open the question-mark menu and choose **Contact CalBudget Support**. This opens the bottom-right live support widget.

You can also use the support launcher in the bottom-right corner when it is visible.

## What support can help with

Support can help with:

- Sign-in and account access.
- Billing and plan status.
- Calendar display issues.
- Transaction entry or recurring rules.
- Imports and reconciliation.
- Advisor questions.
- Mobile or desktop app issues.
- Bugs or unexpected errors.

## What to include

Include enough context to reproduce the issue:

- The page or feature you were using.
- The account or month involved.
- The steps you took.
- Any error message.
- Whether refreshing changes the result.

Do not send bank passwords, full card numbers, full account numbers, or private credentials.

## Support email

You can email [support@calbudget.com](mailto:support@calbudget.com). In-app support is usually better when you are already looking at the problem.
