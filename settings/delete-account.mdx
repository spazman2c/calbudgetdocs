---
title: "Delete your account"
description: "Understand what permanent account deletion removes and how to request it from CalBudget Settings."
---

Account deletion permanently removes your consumer CalBudget account data. This is different from deleting a budget account, canceling a subscription, or resetting the product walkthrough.

<Warning>
  Account deletion cannot be undone. Export or copy anything you need before deleting your account.
</Warning>

## What deletion removes

Deleting your account removes data tied to your CalBudget user record, including:

- Profile
- Budget accounts
- Categories
- Transactions
- Bookmarks
- Debt planner data
- Support tickets
- Feedback
- Onboarding events
- Queued emails
- Billing customer record

CalBudget also attempts to remove linked external access that belongs to the account, including website billing records and connected Plaid access when present.

## Before you delete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cancel or review billing">
    If you subscribed through the website, open Settings, then Billing, and review the Stripe subscription. If you subscribed through the App Store, manage that subscription in Apple's subscription settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save anything you need">
    Account deletion is permanent. Keep any records you need before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support if billing or bank cleanup fails">
    If CalBudget cannot cancel billing or disconnect Plaid automatically, the app will ask you to contact support before deletion finishes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delete from Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the calendar, open the profile menu and choose Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Account">
    Choose the Account tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with your password">
    Enter your current password.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Type "DELETE"'>
    Type `DELETE` exactly as shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete permanently">
    Choose Delete my account permanently. CalBudget removes the account data, signs you out, and returns you to the home page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What deletion does not mean

Deleting your CalBudget account is not the same as:

- Deleting one budget account from the calendar
- Canceling only a subscription while keeping your data
- Hiding Navigator or turning off a setting
- Deleting a CalBudget Business workspace

<Note>
  CalBudget Business workspaces are handled separately from personal CalBudget accounts. Contact support if you need help with a business workspace.
</Note>
