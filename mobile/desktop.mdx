The CalBudget desktop app gives you an app-like way to open the authenticated CalBudget experience on your computer.

## What you can do

The desktop app supports the same core planning workflows as the web app:

- Open your calendar.
- Add and edit transactions.
- Review reports.
- Use Advisor where available.
- Manage settings and billing.
- Contact support.

## Sign in

Open the desktop app and sign in with the same CalBudget account you use on web or mobile.

If sign-in opens a browser window for a provider, complete the provider flow and return to the desktop app.

## Troubleshooting

If the desktop app does not load correctly:

1. Quit and reopen the app.
2. Confirm your internet connection.
3. Sign out and sign back in.
4. Try the web app at [calbudget.com](https://www.calbudget.com).
5. Contact support if the problem continues.
