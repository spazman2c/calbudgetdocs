## App Store

CalBudget is available on the App Store:

[Open CalBudget on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/calbudget/id6768733467)

## Supported sign-in methods

- Sign in with Apple
- Email and password

Google sign-in is not shown on iOS.

## Main mobile screens

- Login and create account
- Today dashboard
- Calendar
- Transactions
- Settings
- Billing/subscription

## Calendar expectations

The mobile calendar should:

- Fill the phone screen cleanly.
- Keep day numbers and balances readable.
- Show adjacent month days when needed to fill the grid.
- Place daily balance beside or within the day cell.
- Avoid horizontal scrolling.

## Today dashboard

The Today view should show useful information immediately:

- Current date context
- Balance summary
- Graphs or charts
- Lowest future balance
- Upcoming transactions
- Quick actions

## Subscriptions

iOS subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can review or cancel an iOS subscription from your Apple ID subscription settings.
