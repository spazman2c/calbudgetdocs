CalBudget 2.1 focuses on polish, clarity, and reliability across the calendar, support experience, iOS app, reports, billing access, and Help Center. This update keeps the same calendar-first planning model while making more of the product feel consistent and easier to use.

## New CalBudget branding

CalBudget has a refreshed visual identity across the website, platform, and iOS app.

- New CalBudget logo and app icon
- Updated web icons and app icons
- More consistent logo placement across navigation, footer, and product surfaces
- Cleaner brand presentation in product screenshots and docs

## Sign-up and checkout

The shared sign-up and sign-in experience has been polished so it is easier to choose the right plan and continue into CalBudget.

- Cleaner sign-up and sign-in modal
- Plan selection for Free, Plus, and Navigator during sign-up
- Improved handoff from account creation to secure Stripe checkout for paid plans
- Better handling when customers need to return to checkout after signing in

## Support page

The public Support page now matches the dark CalBudget experience and works more like a direct support contact page.

- Dark-mode support page design
- Clearer form fields and contrast
- Messages sent directly to `support@calbudget.com`
- Simpler flow for account access, billing, import, calendar, bug report, and feature request questions

## Live chat and help access

Support access has been refined so customers can reach help more naturally from CalBudget.

- Improved support widget behavior
- Better help and support entry points
- More reliable support behavior for signed-in customers
- Clearer path to customer support from Help areas

## Navigator updates

Navigator continues to become a more useful planning companion inside CalBudget.

- Improved Navigator placement and behavior
- Cleaner customer setting for showing or hiding Navigator
- Continued polishing for Navigator as an in-product planning assistant
- Better alignment with the calendar-first planning workflow

## Reports and insights

Reports received new planning visibility around recurring cash-flow patterns.

- New Recurring Balances report experience
- Improved report navigation
- Easier access to recurring balance insights from the platform
- Better visibility into repeated payments, deposits, and balance movement

## Calendar and transactions

The calendar and transaction experience received several usability improvements.

- Improved transaction creation and editing flows
- Better form handling for accounts, categories, and transaction details
- Refined calendar interaction behavior
- Continued polish around day-level planning and transaction management

## iOS app

The iOS app received brand and usability updates.

- New CalBudget app icon assets
- Improved mobile live chat reliability
- Refined iOS settings and appearance behavior
- Updates to parts of the iOS transaction editing experience

## Help Center and docs

The Help Center content has been refreshed with more customer-facing guidance and updated visuals.

- Getting started
- Calendar usage
- Adding and editing transactions
- Recurring transactions
- Bookmarks
- Accounts and categories
- Importing statements
- Reports
- Debt payoff planning
- Navigator
- Plans and billing
- Contacting support

## Business platform

CalBudget Business also received customer-facing presentation updates.

- Improved Business platform pages and visuals
- Updated Business calendar and product screenshots
- More customer-facing guidance for Business workflows

## Billing and subscription access

Billing and subscription access were tightened so customers are more likely to see the right plan state after paying or managing subscriptions.

- Improved subscription handling across website and App Store billing paths
- Better plan/status reliability for paid customers
- More consistent account and subscription access behavior
