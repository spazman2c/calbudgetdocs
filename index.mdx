CalBudget is a calendar-first budgeting app. You place money events on the dates they affect an account, then CalBudget projects the running balance day by day so you can see what is coming before it happens.

## What CalBudget helps you answer

- **Will my account stay safe before the next paycheck?**
- **Which day is my lowest future balance?**
- **What bills, subscriptions, or debt payments create tight timing?**
- **What can I move before the cash-flow problem happens?**
- **Which plan has the features I need?**

## Product surfaces

| Surface | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Web app** | Main calendar, transactions, reports, debt planner, Advisor, billing, support, and settings. |
| **iOS app** | Mobile today view, calendar, transaction entry, settings, and App Store subscription management. |
| **Desktop app** | App-like access to the authenticated CalBudget web experience on desktop. |
| **Docs** | Public help articles at [docs.calbudget.com](https://docs.calbudget.com). |
| **Support widget** | In-app live support from the bottom-right widget. |

## Plans

| Plan | Best for | Includes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Free** | Trying CalBudget and building a first forecast | One account, limited transactions, limited recurring series, limited bookmarks, categories, and manual calendar planning. |
| **Plus** | Unlimited personal planning | Unlimited core planning plus imports, reports, debt tools, advanced features, and AI recommendations. |
| **Advisor** | Guided planning and shared access | Everything in Plus, plus Advisor chat, weekly checkups, action plans, reminders, and shared access roles. |

<Note>
  CalBudget is an informational planning tool, not financial, legal, tax, credit, or investment advice.
