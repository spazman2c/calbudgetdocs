CalBudget is a calendar-first budgeting platform. You place money events on the dates they affect an account, then CalBudget projects the running balance day by day.

Use these docs to set up your first forecast, understand the product model, manage billing, work with Advisor, support customers, and operate the admin tools.

## What CalBudget helps people answer

- **Will my account stay safe before the next paycheck?**
- **Which day is my lowest future balance?**
- **What bills, subscriptions, or debt payments create tight timing?**
- **What can I move before the cash-flow problem happens?**
- **Which plan limits or subscription status apply to this account?**

## Product surfaces

| Surface | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Web app | Main calendar, planning, transactions, reports, debt, Advisor, support, billing, and settings. |
| iOS app | Mobile calendar, today view, transactions, settings, subscriptions, and Apple authentication. |
| Desktop app | Mac and Windows shell focused on login and the authenticated calendar experience. |
| Marketing site | Public homepage, pricing, feature pages, blog, legal pages, and SEO content. |

| Admin panel | Internal operations for users, subscriptions, email, tickets, invoices, content, and audit history. |

## Current plan model

| Plan | Best for | Includes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | Trying the product and building a first forecast | 1 account, 50 lifetime transactions, 5 recurring series, 3 bookmarks, manual calendar planning, and basic categories. |
| Plus | Unlimited personal planning | Unlimited core planning, imports, reports, debt tools, advanced features, and AI recommendations. |
| Advisor | Guided planning and shared access | Everything in Plus, plus Advisor chat, weekly checkups, generated action plans, reminder emails, and shared budget roles. |
