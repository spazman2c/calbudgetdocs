CalBudget has three consumer plans: Free, Plus, and Navigator. Free keeps the core calendar available, Plus removes planning limits and unlocks deeper tools, and Navigator adds guided AI planning on top of Plus.

## What happens on Free

Free is meant for getting a real forecast started. When you reach a Free limit, CalBudget will ask you to upgrade before adding more of that item.

Free limits are:

- 1 budget account
- 50 lifetime transactions
- 5 recurring series
- 3 bookmarks

Your existing data stays connected to your account. Upgrading to Plus or Navigator removes those usage limits.

## Manage a website subscription

Website subscriptions are billed through Stripe.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    From the calendar, open the profile menu and choose Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Open the Billing tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage subscription">
    Choose Manage subscription to open the billing portal for renewal dates, payment method changes, invoices, cancellation, or plan changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also open `/billing`; CalBudget redirects that page to the current subscription screen.

## Manage an App Store subscription

If you subscribed through the iPhone app or App Store, manage the subscription in Apple's subscription settings. Use Restore Purchases in the iOS app if your App Store subscription is active but CalBudget has not updated yet.

<Tip>
  Use the same billing path where you first subscribed. Website billing is managed through Stripe; App Store billing is managed through Apple.
</Tip>

## When billing needs attention

CalBudget may show a billing reminder when a trial is ending, no payment method is on file, or a payment cannot be collected. Open Settings, then Billing, to add a payment method or manage the current subscription.

If your access looks wrong after updating billing, refresh CalBudget first. If the issue continues, contact support with the email address on your CalBudget account and the billing path you used.

<Warning>
  Do not start a second subscription from the other billing path while support is reviewing an access issue. Tell support whether you subscribed through the website or the App Store.
</Warning>
