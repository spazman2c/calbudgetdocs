CalBudget sends platform emails through Resend.

## Email address

Support and reply-to email:

[support@calbudget.com](mailto:support@calbudget.com)

## Email types

- Password reset
- Support ticket opened
- Support ticket reply
- Billing and subscription events
- Trial ended or plan notifications
- Advisor checkups
- Advisor action reminders
- Shared budget invites
- Drip and lifecycle emails
- Newsletter or product updates when opted in

## Email event tracking

Email events store:

- Status
- Scheduled time
- Sent time
- Failure time
- Attempt count
- Last error
- Resend message ID
- Delivery timestamp
- Open timestamp and count
- Click timestamp and count
- Last clicked URL

## Retry behavior

Failed emails can be retried from the admin email page when the platform has enough event detail.

## Webhooks

Resend webhooks update delivery, open, and click tracking. Webhook events should be idempotent.

## Operational rule

Do not send customer platform email from personal Gmail. Use Resend-backed platform email so the admin log and tracking stay accurate.
