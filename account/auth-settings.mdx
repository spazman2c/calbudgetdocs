CalBudget supports account creation and sign-in across web, iOS, and desktop.

## Web authentication

The web app supports:

- Email and password
- Google sign-in
- Password reset
- Session-based authenticated app access

Password reset is for email/password accounts. Google accounts do not need a CalBudget password reset.

## Mobile authentication

The iOS app supports:

- Sign in with Apple
- Email and password

Google sign-in was removed from the iOS app by product decision.

## Password requirements

Passwords must include:

- At least 10 characters
- An uppercase letter
- A lowercase letter
- A number
- A special character

## Password reset flow

1. Choose **Forgot password**.
2. Enter the email address for your CalBudget account.
3. Open the reset email.
4. Choose a new password that meets the requirements.
5. Return to CalBudget and sign in again.

## Account deletion

Apps that support account creation must support account deletion. CalBudget provides account deletion controls in settings.

## Sign-in safety

CalBudget support will never ask you to email your password. If you need help signing in, use the password reset flow or contact support without sharing private credentials.
