CalBudget is designed around manual-first planning. Users can build a budget forecast without connecting bank credentials.

## Privacy principles

- Bank login is not required for core planning.
- Manual data entry is supported on every plan.
- Imports are reviewed before affecting the calendar.
- AI recommendations are opt-in.
- Account deletion is available from settings.

## Account deletion requirements

When a user deletes their account, the flow should:

- Confirm intent before deleting.
- Delete user-owned planning data.
- Avoid requiring customer support to complete deletion.
- Clearly explain that deletion is permanent.

## Data the app stores

Depending on usage, CalBudget may store:

- Account and transaction data
- Categories and bookmarks
- Recurring rules
- Imported statement rows
- Subscription metadata
- Support tickets
- Email delivery events
- Advisor conversations and actions
- Shared access memberships
