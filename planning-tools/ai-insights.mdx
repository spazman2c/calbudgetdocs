CalBudget has two AI-related surfaces:

- **AI recommendations** for paid users who opt in.
- **Advisor** for users on the Advisor plan.

## AI recommendations

AI recommendations create an informational cash-flow summary from recent transaction and forecast data.

Users must opt in from settings. The feature is off by default.

## What AI can analyze

Depending on available data, AI can summarize:

- Spending categories
- Income and expense trends
- Low-balance risk
- Recurring charges
- Possible timing changes
- Planning habits

## What AI should not claim

AI output should not claim to be:

- Financial advice
- Tax advice
- Legal advice
- Credit counseling
- A guaranteed forecast

## Advisor difference

Advisor is a higher plan with a guided workflow:

- Chat about forecast context.
- Weekly checkups.
- Generated action plans.
- Due-step reminders.
- Shared budget access roles.

AI recommendations are a report. Advisor is an ongoing planning layer.
