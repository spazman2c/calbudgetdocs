Bookmarks are reusable transaction templates. They are best for common items that are not predictable enough to be recurring.

## What bookmarks save

A bookmark can store:

- Title
- Amount
- Account
- Category
- Notes

## When to use a bookmark

Use bookmarks for:

- Common grocery trips
- Gas purchases
- Cash withdrawals
- One-off transfers
- Repeated discretionary spending

Do not use bookmarks for fixed bills or paychecks. Those should usually be recurring transactions.

## Applying a bookmark

When creating a new transaction, select a bookmark to fill the form. The date remains the selected calendar date.

CalBudget tracks bookmark use count and last-used time so commonly used templates can appear first.
