Your profile menu is the main place to manage CalBudget. It includes account preferences, billing, theme controls, workspace setup, import tools, reports, support, and sign out.

<img src="/images/settings-support.svg" alt="CalBudget settings and support menu" />

## Profile and preferences

Open your profile menu from the top-right corner of the app. From there you can manage:

- **Profile settings** for name, email-related preferences, appearance, and account controls.
- **Billing and plan** for your current plan, renewal, cancellation, and payment management.
- **Theme** for dark or light mode.
- **Usage** to see how many accounts, transactions, recurring series, and bookmarks you have used.

## Workspace tools

The workspace section helps you organize the forecast:

- **Accounts** for balances and separate forecasts.
- **Categories** for income and spending labels.
- **Bookmarks** for saved quick-add transaction templates.
- **Bank details** for optional planning context.
- **Import statement** for bringing in statement files when available on your plan.

## Planning and export tools

Depending on your plan, you may also see:

- **AI financial report** for an opt-in planning summary.
- **Debt payoff** for calendar-aware debt planning.
- **Export CSV** for downloading transaction data.
- **Print tidy** and **Print full** for printing a cleaner or more detailed calendar view.

## Help menu

Use the question-mark menu in the platform for help.

| Option | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Help Center** | Opens these docs at [docs.calbudget.com](https://docs.calbudget.com). |
| **Contact CalBudget Support** | Opens the bottom-right live support widget. |
| **Latest Updates** | Shows recent product changes. |
| **Keyboard Shortcuts** | Shows available keyboard controls where supported. |

## Contact support

The fastest path is the in-app support widget. You can also email [support@calbudget.com](mailto:support@calbudget.com).

When asking for help, include:

- What you were trying to do.
- Which account or month you were viewing.
- Whether the issue happens after refreshing.
- Any visible error message.

Do not send bank passwords, full card numbers, full account numbers, or private credentials.
